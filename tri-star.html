Our History

Founded in 1996 by two friends, Robert Procell and Glenn Tisdall, with a long history in restaurant operations and kitchen management, Tri-Star Catering has grown into more than exceptional food, tastefully presented.

Whether you're planning a 250 person cocktail party or a 50 person brunch, we commit to helping create your perfect event while staying aware of your budget. Our team is excited to help with every aspect of planning your perfect event.

Corporate Meal Programs

Increase productivity and morale among your employees by showing your care. We have fully customizable breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snack programs tailored to meet all their dietary needs.

You can count on us to always be on time with our delicious food!

Film and Production Catering

Let us take care of all your craft service, crew meals, specialized needs, and logistics. From simple snacks and meals to elegantly appointed themed buffets, Tri-Star Catering takes the work and worry out of your planning process. We know the importance of a happy cast and crew, along with the importance of punctuality.
